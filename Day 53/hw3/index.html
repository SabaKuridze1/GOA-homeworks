Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs with cheese, spinach, and herbs.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 tablespoon chopped herbs (e.g., chives, parsley)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl. Season the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a pan over medium heat and melt butter or oil in the pan.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour the egg mixture into the heated pan and tilt the pan to distribute the eggs evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Once the eggs are almost set, sprinkle the cheese, spinach, and herbs over the top.
  5. Fold the omelette: Use a spatula to fold one side of the omelette over the fillings.
  6. Remove from heat: Serve immediately with your choice of bread or toast.
