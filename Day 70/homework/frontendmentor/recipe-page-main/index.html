Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick omelette recipe perfect for any meal. It can be made in under 10 minutes and requires only a few basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier omelette.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set on the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully fold the unfilled half over the filled half. Once the omelette is cooked through and golden brown, slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy! Serve immediately.
